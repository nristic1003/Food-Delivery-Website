Mister Wang

To get you closer to Chinese cuisine, we will only list a few interesting facts about it. There are more than 20 thousand meals, the fish are cooked in 13 traditional ways, the guest is a slow place at the head of the table, and in the north of China more rice is consumed than rice which is contrary to the habits of people in the south of the country. Also, you have certainly noticed that almost all Chinese food is chopped into pieces and that the Chinese skillfully use wands, which is not a miracle given that more than 45 billion of this equipment is used annually.

But what is perhaps the most striking is the combination of taste that dominates, and it's sweet, sour, angry, salty and bitter. And all these tastes should be balanced in combination to make the meal perfectly tasty and healthy. Well, that's our goal!
