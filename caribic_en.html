Caribic Pizza

We started as a small family business, so that after 27 years of existence, we could proudly stand in front of 13 Caribic pizzas in Belgrade, Novi Sad and Nis. We have become synonymous with unique experience, and always fresh Caribic pizza has won the most demanding.

Caribic pizza is daily dedicated to maintaining the quality of its products and further innovations. From basic foods to the final product - everything is always fresh, of the finest ingredients, with the same recognizable taste and style.

In 2011, we created a unique ice cream revolution, with a completely new concept of enjoying this dessert: our Ice Point. Sweet and refreshing ice cream dessert that daily inspires to combine delicious ingredients for a unique enjoyment in the ice cream aroma of life.
