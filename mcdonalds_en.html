McDonalds

Everything started on a nice day in Belgrade. On March 24, 1988, the first McDonald's restaurant in Serbia was opened in Slavia, and at the same time it was the first in Central and Southeastern Europe. Thus, Belgrade was the first in this part of Europe to enter the world McDonald's map.

From the very beginning, our citizens have recognized the true quality and incomparable service. In McDonald's restaurants, in Serbia, for twenty-nine years, millions of guests have been served and more than twenty million eats of Big Mac ™ have been eaten.

Today in Serbia there are a total of twenty-seven McDonald's restaurants, fifteen in Belgrade, four in Novi Sad, two in Nis and one in Subotica, Kragujevac, Velika Plana, Pancevo, Stara Pazova and Borca. McDonald's is among the 300 most successful companies in Serbia for 2009, according to the magazine "Ekonomist".
